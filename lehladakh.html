About Camp

Here is the Detailed Structure of the Camp.

  • Day 1

    Depart from Surat

    Journey towards Jammu starts from Surat on the date of departure. Reporting time will depends on the mode of transportation from Surat. That can be bit flexible.

  • Day 2

    Arrival at Jammu in the Evening

    After arrival at Jammu in the evening. Hotel check-in and take a rest. Night stay at Jammu

  • Day 3

    Jammu to Srinagar

    After breakfast Departure from Jammu to Srinagar on the way enjoy the sight seeing. Hotel Check-in at Srinagar. Overnight stay in Srinagar hotel/ guest house.

  • Day 4

    Srinagar to Kargil

    Enjoy the breakfast and depart from Srinaga to Kargil via Zojila pass and can gaze at the local sightseeing - Sonamarg, Dras War Memorial, Suru Valley. Hotel check-in and enjoy the sizzling dinner. Night Stay in Kargil.

  • Day 5

    Kargil to Leh

    Enjoy the breakfast and then start your most awaited journey - Leh Ladakh via Laimaiguru Moon Land Akai Monastery, Magnetic Hill, Sangam of Indus & Zanskar River. Hotel check-in and nightstay in Leh.

  • Day 6

    Acclimatization Day

    Take the breakfast and after that enjoy the Local Sight seeing of Leh (- Hall of Fame, Santi Stupa, Leh Palace) After Sightseeing return back to hotel. Night stay in Leh.

  • Day 7

    Departure for Nubra valley

    Enjoy the breakfast and depart from Leh towards Nubra. On the way Visit Khardungla Pass( world highest motorable pass), Hundar white Sand Dunes, Disket monastery, Camel safari(self). Night Stay at Nubra.

  • Day 8

    Departure for Pangong

    Departure for pangong lake early morning. After enjoying Pangong Lake depart towards Leh via Chang La Pass (Second Highest Motorable Road) and on the way take a visit to - Rancho School, Shey Monastery & Palace. Night Stay in Leh.

  • Day 9

    Leh market & local sightseeing

    After Breakfast take a rest and enjoy the remaining sight seeing of Leh and take a glance at Leh Market and enjoy Shopping.

  • Day 10

    Free Day for Leh Bike Ride

    Leh free day for bike ride(Rent not included in package). Departure for Srinagar. Overnight journey to Srinagar.

  • Day 11

    Srinagar Sight seeing

    After breakfast Visit Sonamarg. Overnight stay in Srinagar hotel/guest house.

  • Day 12

    Srinagar to Jammu

    After breakfast depart for Jammu. Night stay at Jammu

  • Day 13

    Departure for Hometown

    Wake up, freshen up and then check out Hotel and move towards Railway station. Return Journey to Surat by JAT ADI Express (19224).

  • Day 14

    Back to Home

    Arrival at Hometown with lots of memories.
