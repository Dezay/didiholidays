About Camp

Here is the Detailed Structure of the Camp.

  • Day 1

    Ahmedabad to Delhi (by Air)

    The trip will start from Ahmedabad Airport by morning at 8 o’clock. From here we will board our flight to Delhi and the journey will be around 1.45 hrs. We will reach Delhi airport at around 12:00. After reaching Delhi we will pick our Volvo bus for Kasol.

  • Day 2

    Manali Basecamp Site

    We will arrive at Manali Basecamp by morning. After Breakfast we will rest till Lunch (or you can explore river side area) After Lunch Rope Course Activities, Bamboo bridge, sloth walk, Burma Bridge. Then we will have Tea with Snacks & music with Bone fire. Overnight stay at camp.

  • Day 3

    Manali to Rohlikholi

    Post breakfast, we will drive to Gulaba. From here, we start our 6 km trek to Rohlikholi. Overnight stay in camp.

  • Day 4

    Trek to Bhrigu Lake

    Trek to Early in the morning, we will start on a 5-6 hour drive to Panduropa. After breakfast, we will start our trek to the Bhrigu lake, one of the holiest lakes in the Kullu valley. After spending some time at the lake, we begin our descent to Panduropa. Overnight stay in camp.

  • Day 5

    Leisure Day

    Reserve Day. Just in case, if the weather does not permit the trek on day 5, it can be attempted on day 06. Back to Manali in the morning/evening. Night stay in hotel.

  • Day 6

    Manali to Delhi

    After having Breakfast, we will move for Manali sightseeing. We will visit Vashishta Kund, Hadimba Temple, Van Vihar, Mall Road. By evening at 4:30 we will board Volvo for Delhi.

  • Day 7

    Delhi to Ahmedabad

    We will reach Delhi by morning. You can roam in Delhi city by your own. By late evening we will board the flight to Ahmedabad. The glorious trip ends here.
