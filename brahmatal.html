About Camp

Here is the Detailed Structure of the Camp.

  • Day 1

    Kathgodam to Lohajung

    Reporting at Kathgodam Railway Station at 7:00 A.M. The station is just 25 kms away from Nainital and 9 kms away from a city known as Haldwani. Our representative will receive you from Kathgodam railway station and from there we will drive towards Lohajung(Base Camp). Lohajung is a place where goddess Parvati had a fight (Jung) with a demon called Lohasur. So the place Lohajung literally means ‘Fight with Lohasur’. On the way from Kathgodam to Lohajung you will go through some beautiful locations.

  • Day 2

    Lohajung to Bekaltal / 3-4 hr Trek

    Today we will trek to Bekaltal. During the trek you will pass through the forests of Oak and pine. On the way will come across the beautiful Kail valley and Pindari Rivers. Crossing the bridge over the stream we will stop for a while at Begum a location that offers splendid views of Navali Bugyal. After a 4- 5 hours trek we finally reach Bekaltal. Overnight stay at camps.

  • Day 3

    Bekaltal to Brahmatal- Khabelkal / 3-4 hr trek

    Wake up early in the morning to enjoy the beauty on the nature.Post breakfast we will start trekking towards Khabelkal; on the way to the Advance Base Camp you will go through the dense oak forest and will take us around 3-4 hours to complete the trek. Overnight stay at camps.

  • Day 4

    Brahmatal-Khabelkal to Brahmatal Peak to Daldum / 6-7 hr

    So, today is the 4th day of our adventurous trek and today’s trek is quite long as it will take us up to 7 hours to complete. The trail will take us through the different locations like: Chota and Bada Jhandidar to reach Brahmatal. Reaching Brahmatal peak on your own gives you a sense of achievement. Spending some time there we will trek further towards our campsite Daldum which is around 3 hours away from the peak. Overnight stay will be at camps.

  • Day 5

    Daldum to Lohajung / 4-5 hr

    Today is the last day of our adventure trek, and post breakfast we will head back towards Lohajung and bid adieu to the beautiful Daldum. We will reach Lohajung in around 2-3 hours approximately. Overnight stay will be at camps.

  • Day 6

    Lohajung to Kathgodam 8-9 hr

    We will depart from Lohajung to Kathgodam and say goodbye to all your trek companions who must have become your good friends, pack-up your bags and take back some memorable moments and adventurous memories home with you.
