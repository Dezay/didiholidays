About Camp

Here is the Detailed Structure of the Camp.

  • Day 1

    Depart from Ahmedabad

    Journey towards Pathankot starts from Kalupur Railway Station on the date of departure. Reporting time will be 10:30 am at Kalupur Railway Station.

  • Day 2

    Departure for Dharamshala in the Evening

    In evening you will reach Pathankot and after freshen up depart for Dharamshala (3 hour journey by road from Pathankot). Accommodation will be allotted at the base camp(at Bhagsunag Village). After dinner take rest. .

  • Day 3

    Free Day for Acclimatization

    After the breakfast, get yourself acclimatized by a short walk to Bhagsunag Temple and Waterfall. After lunch, explore the McLeod Ganj Market and take a visit to Dalai Lama Monastery. After Dinner take rest.

  • Day 4

    Trek from Base Camp to Advance Camp

    After Breakfast start your trek for the Advance Base Camp (Triund - 10,632 ft) with the packed lunch from the Base Camp. Have your lunch on the way to advance base camp. After reaching advance base camp and freshenup enjoy the Team Games and Dinner. Night stay at Advance Base Camp.

  • Day 5

    Trek to Summit point and back to Base Camp

    Enjoy the morning with the majestic views bestowed upon by Nature. After Breakfast, cross over Indrahar-Pass Jot (located at height of about 4,342 meters i.e 14,245 ft above sea level). Enjoy Lunch after reaching the summit point, Free time for photos, back to Base Camp. Dinner and Campfire, Night stay at Base Camp.

  • Day 6

    Dharamshala Local Sightseeing

    After Breakfast get ready to experience the thrill of paragliding. Gyuto Monastery and back to Hotel for Lunch. After Lunch take a visit at War Memorial, Tea Garden, Dal Lake, Naddi Village (Enjoy the sunset). Dinner Night stay at Base Camp.

  • Day 7

    Dharamshala Local Sightseeing

    After lunch you will leave the hotel. On the way to Pathankot visit HPCA Stadium and Kangra Fort. Drive to Pathankot and night stay at Pathankot.

  • Day 8

    Departure for Ahmedabad

    Wake up, freshen up and then check out Hotel and move towards Railway station. Return Journey to Ahmedabad by JAT ADI Express (19224)

  • Day 9

    Back to Home

    Arrival at Ahmedabad with lots of memories.
